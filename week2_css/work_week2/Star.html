⭐ 7.2/10 Rate

“Skeleton Crew” wants to remind us of the early, kid-focused days of the Star Wars saga. The story finds four young people trying to get back to their home planet after stumbling upon a buried Jedi temple, then getting whisked away into the galaxy. Fans are trying to figure out how Jude Law's character Jod will factor into the story since he's not canon, but he appears to be Force-sensitive in the first trailer. We might feel like we're hanging over a precipice if it turns out to be one-and-done for the series.
